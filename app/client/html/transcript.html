Additional Notes

Since École 42 does not issue official transcripts, this document has been prepared by a student.
The curriculum at 42 is divided into three stages:

  • The “Piscine” (literally “swimming pool”): the entrance exam, during which students spend one full month completing daily projects and taking weekly exams.
  • The Common Core: based on the same principle, but with five exams (Rank 02 to Rank 06 — there are no 00 or 01) spread across the first two years of study.
  • Post-Core (or Specialization): after completing the final Common Core project, Transcendence, students move on to the last stage before the Master's degree, which involves completing additional projects and undertaking two professional experiences.
After fulfilling all requirements, students appear before a jury, which decides whether to award the diploma.
